About Me

Hello! I am John, a graduate of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I have five years of technical support experience, which includes my two years spent as a working student. I've worked with other professionals to provide solutions for companies.

Since I was a kid I am fascinated and had always been interested with computers. I visited computer stores/shops just to fiddle, tinker and see how they operate. I even created HTML webpages after learning some computer programming skills on my own. I fell in love with it more and more and had the urge to pursue it as a career. To reach my goal and further my learnings in computer, I took up the course in college, Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Programming. I chose to major in programming in order to gain more technical knowledge. Moreover, I applied to be a working student or member of the technical support staff at our institution, dealing with both software and hardware environments. With this, I considered myself to be fairly skilled in this field given my experience. However, I can still push myself to increase my learning through the following; experimenting with new technologies, doing further study and research in this area.
